- Valid email address

Peacock TV Login Process
========================

Signing in to your Peacock TV account is simple and secure.

Access the Sign In Page
-----------------------

Navigate to the official Peacock TV website or open the Peacock TV app on your device. Select the **Sign In** option.

Enter Your Credentials
----------------------

Input your registered email address and password. Ensure that the information is typed correctly and that caps lock is disabled if not required.

Click the **Sign In** button to access your account dashboard.

Start Streaming
---------------

After successful login, you can browse content categories, search for your favorite shows, and start streaming immediately.

How to Activate Peacock TV on Smart Devices
===========================================

If you are using a smart television or streaming device, activation may be required.

Install the Peacock TV App
--------------------------

Download and install the Peacock TV application from your device app store.

Open the App
------------

Launch the app and select the sign in option. An activation code may appear on your screen.

Visit the Activation Page
-------------------------

On a separate device such as a computer or smartphone, open a web browser and go to the official Peacock TV activation page.

Enter the Activation Code
-------------------------

Type the code displayed on your television screen into the activation field and confirm.

Once verified, your device will refresh automatically and you can begin streaming.

How to Reset Peacock TV Password
=================================
